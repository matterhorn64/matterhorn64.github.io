If At First You Don't Succeed, Iterate Again

Android: Netrunner. A competitive card game on its third incarnation, currently piloted by a grassroots fan organization. Netrunner is a game of:

  • Timing
  • Asymmetry
  • Bluffing
  • Risk

    • The game asks a lot of its new players, but at its best is a thrill ride that will pull you in deeper each time you play. Constructing your own deck and piloting it against your opponents is a joy, and learning to tune it opens up new opportunities to improve. I wholeheartedly recommend the game to anyone with any interest in cyberpunk.
